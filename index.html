What is JidouNavi?

JidouNavi (from Japanese "jidouhanbaiki" meaning vending machine) is a mobile app that helps you discover Japan's incredible variety of vending machines. With over 5 million vending machines across Japan - that's roughly one for every 23 people - there's always something interesting around the corner.

Types of Vending Machines in Japan

Japan's vending machines go far beyond drinks and snacks. JidouNavi helps you find:

  • Weird & Unusual: Hot ramen, canned bread, rhinoceros beetles, umbrellas, and fresh eggs
  • Retro Machines: Classic designs from the Showa era, still dispensing nostalgia
  • Gachapon: Capsule toy machines with everything from anime figures to tiny furniture
  • Regional Specialties: Local treats only available in specific areas
  • 24/7 Hot Food: Udon, takoyaki, even full meals at any hour

Frequently Asked Questions

Is JidouNavi free?

Yes! JidouNavi is completely free to download and use. All core features are available at no cost.

Does it work outside Japan?

JidouNavi is focused on Japan, where vending machine culture is most vibrant. We may expand to other countries in the future based on community interest.

How do I add a vending machine?

When you're near a machine, tap the "Add Machine" button, take a photo, select categories, and submit. Your contribution helps fellow explorers!

What are badges?

Badges are achievements you earn by visiting machines, contributing to the map, and exploring different categories. From "First Find" to "Vending Sensei" - there are dozens to collect.
